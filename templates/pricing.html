Questions

What a credit covers.

What's a credit worth?

A credit is one GPU-minute on an A100 baseline. A BindCraft pilot reserves 22 credits (≈ 45 min on A100-80GB); if it finishes in 30 min, 7 credits refund to your ledger. Smoke runs are 2 to 8 credits.

What happens if a job fails?

Pre-flight input validation runs before GPU time is consumed. If the preflight rejects, zero credits are charged. If the GPU job itself fails mid-run, any unused cap refunds — you pay only for compute actually used.

Can I run on my own GPUs?

Not today. Every tool runs on Modal infrastructure we manage and validate — that's the tradeoff for published audit logs and predictable runtimes. For self-hosted workflows, the underlying models (RFdiffusion, RFantibody, BoltzGen) are open source.
