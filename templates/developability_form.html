What you get back

A composite 0 to 1 score plus five per-dimension breakdowns. Each flagged motif is returned with its 1-based position, severity, and whether it falls in an approximated CDR. The sequence is rendered with liability positions highlighted.

This is a prototype scorer. CDR boundaries are estimated from length; production annotation via ANARCI with IMGT numbering is on the roadmap. Humanness uses a small germline panel; we are migrating to the full IMGT repertoire and OASis-style k-mer scoring.
