About ESMFold

What it is

ESMFold (Lin et al., Science 2023). A single-sequence structure predictor built on Meta's ESM-2 3B protein language model. No MSA, no templates - the fold comes directly from the language model's internal representation. Much faster than AF2 (30 s vs 5-10 min) at a modest accuracy cost on well-studied families; competitive to better on orphans and novel sequences. Repo: facebookresearch/esm.

When to use

Pick ESMFold when you need the fastest possible fold for a monomer - screening designs, a quick sanity-check, or a fold for a sequence AF2 struggles with (few homologs). For multimers use ColabFold (D3) or AF2 (D2). For full-accuracy work on well-studied families use AF2 standalone with MSA.

Inputs

  • FASTA - single chain only, 10-400 aa.

Outputs

  • Predicted structure (PDB)
  • Per-residue pLDDT (0-100)
  • pTM (when the checkpoint exposes it)
