About BindCraft

What it wraps

BindCraft (Pacesa et al., bioRxiv 2024.09.30.615802). AlphaFold2-Multimer backbone design with hotspot-focused backpropagation, followed by ProteinMPNN sequence design and AF2 re-prediction filtering. Repo: martinpacesa/BindCraft.

When to pick BindCraft

Pick BindCraft when you have a target PDB plus known hotspot residues and want de novo 60-150 aa protein binders. For VHH/scFv antibody scaffolds, use RFantibody. For targets with glycans or non-canonical residues, use BoltzGen.

Prerequisites

  • Target structure in .pdb, .cif, or .mmcif format.
  • Chain ID of the target within that structure.
  • At least one hotspot residue index on the target chain.

What the inputs mean

  • Hotspot residues: comma-separated target-chain residue indices the binder should contact. These bias the AF2 backpropagation toward the intended epitope.
  • Binder length (min/max): residue-count window for the generated binder chain (50-150).
  • Num designs: how many final filtered designs are returned. Each passes AF2 re-prediction with ipTM and pLDDT above the BindCraft default thresholds.
  • Seed: RNG seed for reproducible sampling; set automatically per run.
