About PXDesign

What it wraps

Ranomics in-house binder-design pipeline. An RFdiffusion-style backbone generator proposes binders against the target, and every candidate is re-scored with JAX AlphaFold2 run in Initial Guess mode (AF2-IG). Reported ipTM, pLDDT, and pAE come from the AF2 monomer model, not from the generator.

Scoring stage: Bennett et al., Nat. Commun. 14, 2625 (2023). Paper. Pipeline source is private (Kendrew).

When to pick PXDesign

Pick PXDesign when AF2 confidence against a defined target matters and you want real ipTM, pLDDT, and pAE on every candidate. For hallucination-driven binder design without AF2 filtering use BindCraft, for antibody and nanobody CDRs use RFantibody, and for target structure generation without binder design use BoltzGen.

Prerequisites

  • An account tier with the credit balance listed below.
  • For the pilot tier: a target structure in .pdb, .cif, or .mmcif format, the target chain ID, and at least one hotspot residue index on that chain.
  • For smoke and preview tiers: nothing — both run against the baked PD-L1 IgV reference target.

What the inputs mean

  • Preset: smoke skips the post-design filter, preview adds it against the PD-L1 reference, pilot runs the full post-filtered pipeline against your uploaded target.
  • Binder length: residue count of the generated binder chain (40–150).
  • Target chain: chain ID in the uploaded structure the binder should engage.
  • Hotspot residues: comma-separated residue indices on the target chain used as contact bias for the generator.
  • Number of designs: how many independent backbones to generate and score (1–5).
